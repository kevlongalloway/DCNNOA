President's Message

Welcome to DCNNOA

"Our people are the asymmetrical advantage our services bring in all domains."

For the past 250 years, the strength of our sea services has been central to our national security. NNOA has been a key stakeholder in recruiting, developing, and retaining the best, most capable officer corps in the Navy, Marine Corps, and Coast Guard.

DCNNOA is a premiere chapter that relentlessly promotes talent development through our annual Scholarship Program — $1.15M awarded to 671 students since 2003 — and our annual VA Hospital Visit with over $5K donated per year for essential clothing items.

Your NNOA Membership will contribute to the competitive advantage needed for the next 250 years — a sea service that can recruit and retain the nation's top talent to lead across all domains.

The DCNNOA Scholarship program is open annually to DMV high school seniors. Since its founding, the program has supported 671 students pursuing education and careers in service to their country.

Chapter Events

11 Nov 2025

Annual Veterans Day Hospital Visit

DCNNOA and VFW Post 9619 conducted their annual visit to the Washington DC Veterans Hospital. Eighteen chapter members spent the morning meeting, greeting, and thanking veterans receiving medical care.

Members provided socks, knit caps, gloves, leggings, and thermal underwear to veterans in need.

Our History

Service Timeline

1972

NNOA Founded

Established to support minority naval officers and promote diversity in the Sea Services.

2003

Scholarship Launched

DCNNOA launches its annual program for DMV high school seniors, beginning a legacy of investment.

2010

VA Hospital Tradition

The annual Veterans Day hospital visit becomes a hallmark DCNNOA community event.

2020

Virtual Operations

Chapter meetings transition to Zoom, ensuring continuity of mission during unprecedented times.
